Movement is a Farcaster Frame for social activism, where users can (anonymously) create, share, and sign petitions. 

Features:
1. Create new petition
1a. Upload image
2. Sign ongoing petition
3. Donate (ERC-20)
4. Account abstraction
5. Web2 sign-in
